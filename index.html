Peter's Gym

Founded by KP Peter in 1980, Peter's Gym started its journey with its first branch in Thrissur. Now Peter's Gym is renowned as a premium class gym Brand in India. With 6+ branches across the country, the entity is setting a benchmark in the fitness industry in terms of facilities, quality trainers and best service. Few more branches are about to open soon with a mission to deliver quality training and activities for the health of people.
Peter's Sports Nutrition and Volterix Gym Equipments are the other 2 brands under Peter's Group. We have got 42 years of history behind us and a very bright future ahead of us. No other gym concepts can rival the brand strength of Peter's gym.
Peter's Gym Thrissur is the largest gym in the Thrissur district ever. Spread in 9000+ sqft area, the gym is all set with quality machines and equipments, certified professional trainers, special activities, good ambiance and the quality service we deliver.
